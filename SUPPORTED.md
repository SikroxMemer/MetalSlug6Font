## Font 1 Support

Font 1 provides support for a wide range of characters, including:

### Letters

- Lowercase and Uppercase Letters

### Numbers

- Numerals from 0 to 9

### Symbols

- Apostrophe (')
- Asterisk (*)
- Braces (Curly Brackets) - `{}`, `{}`, `()`
- Brackets (Square Brackets) - `[]`, `[]`, `()`
- Caret (^)
- Colon ( : )
- Dollar Sign ($)
- Equals (=)
- Exclamation Mark (!)
- Greater-than Sign (>)
- Hyphen (-)
- Infinity (âˆž)
- Less-than Sign (<)
- Number Sign (#)
- Percent Sign (%)
- Period (.)
- Plus Sign (+)

- Question Mark (?)
- Quotation Marks - Double ("), Single ('), and Backtick (`)
- Semicolon ( ; )
- Slash (/)
- Tilde (~)
- Underscore (_)
- Vertical Bar (|)
